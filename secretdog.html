The Portfolio of Matthew Verge

Corndogs are more than you think they are. You may worry about your worthfulness in the fleeting "beauty" that is life. Everyone has reached a point in their life where they stop and truly question "why?". Not to anything in particular, but rather to specifically, everything.
Why is it so hard to make friends after college? Why does it feel like time is passing faster the older you become?

These questions become redundant when you think about them. You have lost friends, family, and ideas.
You have lost hope in many aspects of life, and yet you push on. Why is that?


Why, if it is so easy to give in to all the easy ways out, do so many people trudge through the no-longer-imaginary cacotopia that is the Earth?
This site will offer no answer. Even now, it would seem like this space of text on a screen is doing naught but filling your head with negative emotions. The answer to life's most grueling questions, about human conflict, the disgusting behaviour of some people on the planet.

Humans can take complete 180 nosedives in opinions, hide their true motives their entire lives.
What of me? Am I simply reguritating more of the same said by all, views widely agreed upon that determine what is wrong? Perhaps.
You're not going to find what you're looking for here; on a page written by a person with so little experience in life, who claims to have been thrust into emotional maturity earlier than expected.


However, at the end of the day, not finding what you want on this website might be a good thing. This corn dog experience might provide what you need, instead.
